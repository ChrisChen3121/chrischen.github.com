2 Elasticities

2.1 Price Elasticity

$$\epsilon_x = \frac{\%\Delta Q_x}{\%\Delta P_x}$$
  • Elastic (very responsive) - \(|\epsilon_x| > 1\)
  • Inelastic (not very responsive) - \(|\epsilon_x| < 1\)
  • Unitary (without changes) - \(|\epsilon_x| = 1\)
  • Perfectly Inelastic - \(|\epsilon_x| = 0\)
  • Perfectly Elastic - \(|\epsilon_x| = \infty\)

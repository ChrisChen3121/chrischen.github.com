* Linear Algebra
** Vector
*** Dot project
    $$r\cdot s = \sum_i^n r_i s_i$$
    - commutative
    - distributive $r\cdot (s+t) = r\cdot s+r\cdot t$
    - $r\cdot(as)=a(r\cdot s)$ when $a$ is a scalar
    - $r\cdot r = |r|^2$
*** cos rule
    $$c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2abc\cos\theta$$
    - vectorized
      $$|r-s|^2=|r|^2 + |s|^2 - 2|r||s|\cos\theta$$
      $$r\cdot s=|r||s|\cos\theta$$

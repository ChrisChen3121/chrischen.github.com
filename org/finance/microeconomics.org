* Demand
** Demand Factors
   - $Q_x$: quantity demanded of $x$
   - $P_x$: price of $x$
   - $I$: income
   - $P$: preference
*** $P_r$: price of related good
    - substitute
    - complement
** Demand Function
   $$Q_x = f(P_x, P_r, I, P)$$
